About Company

Quick Construction Pvt. Ltd is registered Construction Company at Company Registrar Office of Kathmandu, Nepal in 2078 B.S. (2021 A.D.).

We are basically working for road construction through tender bid or on contract basis. We have also organized to work for commercial construction and supervision of the site as well as provide project management for any projects of construction, and also work for buildings, bridges, parks, irrigation, cannel, tunnel, and others with a strong brilliant team of Engineers and management. Quick Construction Pvt. Ltd. head office is located at Kathmandu, ward no. - 12, Teku with all managing & technical team on daily work. We do regular plan and preparation for our every site, observe daily, monitor and cure the problem for the site with quick response. We are building an intelligent engineering system to develop a team for high tech construction and planning for infrastruction development of nation and commercial projects. We make a healthy relation with our clients and day to day discuss makes our client satisfaction level better for productive work on site.

Our team also help clients to reduce the extra cost for project which are unnecessary and wastages, also provide a good guideline for the success of project. We do our work from zero level to finish level with our 100% effort and passion for the work.

Quick Construction want to build a construction company which could lead construction to the next level and build a wonderful and advance design, modern structures. Our Aim is to be Nepal no. 1 Company in construction service through our work and engineering.
